Farmhouse AR

Farmhouse AR is a farming game that was developed in 3 weeks for a school assignment.

It was developed in Unreal Engine 4 by me and one artist.

Video

Experience

Prior to making this game, I had zero experience with Unreal and Augmented Reality so it was pretty challenging at the start. AR Support in Unreal Engine is basic and minimal and even though there are documentations available, they do not tell you much other than just placing simple objects and plane tracking. Regardless, it is still possible to develop a fun AR game.
